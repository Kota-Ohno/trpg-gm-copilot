## Hashtag Strategy

Use two or three hashtags. The launch post should not look like a broad recruitment post.

Recommended default:

- `#TRPG`
- `#TRPGツール`
- `#つぎたく`

Why:

- `#TRPG` is the broad category tag and should be present on the first announcement.
- `#TRPGツール` describes the artifact without implying a session recruitment.
- `#つぎたく` gives users a stable tag for feedback, bug reports, screenshots, and follow-up posts.

Optional by context:

- `#TRPG好きと繋がりたい`: use only for a softer community-facing follow-up, not for every product announcement.
- `#オンラインセッション`: use only when the post specifically discusses online-session logs or browser use.
- `#GM`: use sparingly because it is broad outside TRPG and may add noise.

Avoid for the product launch:

- `#PL募集`
- `#卓募集`
- `#TRPG募集`
- System-specific recruitment tags such as `#CoC募集`

Reason: these tags are commonly used for finding or announcing sessions. Using them for a tool launch can attract the wrong intent and may feel like tag hijacking.

Suggested launch combinations:

- First announcement: `#TRPG #TRPGツール #つぎたく`
- Short reminder: `#TRPG #つぎたく`
- Feedback request: `#TRPG #つぎたく`
- Community-facing follow-up: `#TRPG #TRPG好きと繋がりたい #つぎたく`

## Operating Plan

- Launch from the account that can keep replying for the first 24 hours.
- Post with one screenshot or short screen recording if available; otherwise post text-only first and add media in a reply after smoke verification.
- Pin the launch post while the Pages URL is the primary public entry.
- Watch replies for 24 hours, then summarize repeated feedback into GitHub issues or release notes.
- Do not enable analytics, cookies, or server-side logging just for launch feedback unless `PRIVACY.md` is updated first.
- Do not ask users to paste private campaign logs in public replies.
- Avoid aggressive reposting; use one launch post, one explanatory thread, and measured replies.

## Notes From X Help

- Standard posts support text, links, and media, and the regular compose flow says up to 280 characters.
- X lists current technical account limits, including 2,400 posts per day split into smaller intervals.
- Longer posts are an X Premium feature, so the launch copy should work as standard short posts.
- Public TRPG session-search guidance commonly points users to recruitment tags such as `#TRPG募集`, `#PL募集`, and `#卓募集`; these should be avoided for a product launch because they imply session recruitment rather than a tool announcement.
