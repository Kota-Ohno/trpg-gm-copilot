# Service Name Review

Date: 2026-05-09
Status: Draft for decision

## Naming Criteria

- Communicates session-log continuity, not an AI GM replacement.
- Works for Japanese UI while remaining pronounceable in English.
- Feels useful and calm for working GMs, not generic fantasy decoration.
- Can stretch from local-first MVP to hosted sync, visual packs, and team workflows later.
- Avoids names that are too narrow (`Log...` only), too generic (`GM Assistant`), or too fantasy-cliche (`Grimoire`, `Tavern`, `Quest...`).

## Current Name: Loreline

Strengths:

- Compact, memorable, and aligned with campaign continuity.
- Suggests a line of lore from past sessions to future prep.
- Flexible enough for investigation, fantasy, and custom campaigns.

Risks:

- Slightly abstract; first-time users may not immediately infer TRPG utility.
- `Lore` leans fantasy unless surrounding copy anchors it in session logs and GM workflow.
- Availability is not verified here. Trademark, domain, and app-store checks are still required before final adoption.

## Candidate Shortlist

| Name | Read | Fit | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Loreline | ロアライン | Strong | Best current balance of memorability and continuity. Keep if the product copy makes the use case concrete. |
| Tableline | テーブルライン | Strong | More explicitly tabletop. Slightly less elegant, but clearer for TRPG positioning. |
| Sessionline | セッションライン | Medium | Very clear, but more descriptive and less brandable. |
| Loreloom | ロアルーム | Medium | Good continuity/craft metaphor, but pronunciation may be confused with `room` in Japanese. |
| Plotline GM | プロットラインGM | Medium | Clearer story prep signal, but `Plotline` is common and more writing-tool coded. |
| Threadmark | スレッドマーク | Medium | Good for clues/continuity, but less obviously tabletop and may feel too task-management-like. |
| Session Loom | セッションルーム | Weak | English phrase is nice, but Japanese reading collides with `room`. |
| Campaign Cartographer | キャンペーンカートグラファー | Weak | Strong concept, too long and likely conflicts with existing tabletop tools. |

## Recommendation

Keep `Loreline` as the working product name for this branch, but tighten the surrounding copy:

- Product category: `GM Continuity Studio`
- Japanese promise: `前回のログを、次回の卓へ。`
- Functional descriptor: `セッションログから記憶、準備、PL共有を作るGM作業台`

If we want a clearer name before public launch, the strongest alternate is `Tableline`. It trades some elegance for immediate tabletop comprehension.

## Decision Needed

- Option A: Keep `Loreline` and improve copy only.
- Option B: Rename to `Tableline` before public launch.
- Option C: Run availability research first, then decide between `Loreline`, `Tableline`, and one new round of candidates.
