# Terms Of Use

Last updated: 2026-05-09

These terms are a practical launch baseline for `つぎたく`. They are not a substitute for jurisdiction-specific legal review before a commercial launch.

## Service Boundary

`つぎたく` is a support tool for human GMs. It is not an AI GM, rules authority, safety moderator, legal advisor, or data backup service. AI/provider output is draft material until the GM reviews and approves it.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- Choosing what campaign logs, audio, secrets, and player information to enter.
- Confirming they have permission to process or share table content.
- Reviewing provider privacy terms before sending content to OpenAI, Ollama, browser speech recognition, or any custom endpoint.
- Keeping backups of important campaign data.
- Protecting their own provider API keys.

## No Hosted Data Recovery

The initial public app is static and local-first. It does not provide a hosted account, server-side database, or recovery service. Browser storage can be cleared by the user, browser, device policy, or storage pressure.

## No Warranty

The app is provided as-is. It may contain mistakes, incomplete output, provider errors, browser storage limits, or compatibility issues. Use it at your own risk and verify outputs before using them at the table.

## External Providers

Provider charges, rate limits, account status, safety policies, logs, model behavior, and data handling are controlled by the selected provider. `つぎたく` does not control those provider-side terms.

## Changes

These terms may change before or after public launch. Material changes should be recorded in release notes or the repository history.
